My Journey

I'm Muhammad Umair Nasir, a passionate DevOps Engineer and Cloud Specialist graduate from Lahore Garrison University. With over 4 years of hands-on experience in the field, I've developed a deep expertise in containerization, cloud infrastructure, and automation.

My journey in tech started with a curiosity about how systems work, which evolved into a career focused on building scalable, secure, and efficient infrastructure. I specialize in Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD pipelines, and cloud platforms like AWS and GCP.

What sets me apart is my unique combination of skills in DevOps, Cloud Computing, and Digital Forensics. This allows me to not only build robust infrastructure but also ensure its security and integrity through forensic analysis and monitoring.

I believe in continuous learning and staying updated with the latest technologies. Currently, I'm exploring advanced Kubernetes patterns, service mesh architectures, and cloud-native security solutions.
